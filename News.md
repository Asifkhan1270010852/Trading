## News Trading

1. CPI, (The Consumer Price Index) Yeh ek economic indicator hai jo batata hai ki ek aam consumer ke kharche (goods & services) ki prices badh rahi hain ya ghat rahi hain. CPI measure karta hai inflation — yaani price increase of daily-use items. CPI batata hai inflation level in an economy. Agar CPI badhta hai → prices badh rahe hain → inflation high. Agar CPI girta hai → prices gir rahe hain → inflation low.
   
2. PPI, (Producer Price Index) Yeh measure karta hai: Producers (manufacturers, wholesalers) ke liye goods aur services ke price change ko.. Simple words me: CPI consumer ke liye hota hai, PPI producers (ya businesses) ke liye hota hai.

3. Manufacturing PMI, (Purchasing Managers’ Index) Manufacturing PMI ek survey-based indicator hai jo measure karta hai ki manufacturing sector (factory production) grow kar raha hai ya slow down ho raha hai. Simple Word: Manufacturing PMI batata hai ki factories aur industries me “production, new orders, employment aur demand” badh rahi hai ya ghat rahi hai.

4. Service PMI, Services PMI (Purchasing Managers’ Index) ek survey-based index hai jo measure karta hai ki services sector (jaise IT, banking, hotels, transport, healthcare, etc.) expand ho raha hai ya contract (ghat) ho raha hai. Simple words me 👇 Services PMI batata hai ki ek desh me “non-manufacturing” ya “service-based” business activity badh rahi hai ya kam ho rahi hai.

5. NFP, (Non-Farm Payrolls) ek monthly U.S. employment report hai jo batata hai ki U.S. economy me pichle month me kitni naukriyan (jobs) create hui hain — agriculture (farm) sector ke alawa.. Agriculture ke alawa sab industries — jaise manufacturing, construction, retail, healthcare, IT, hospitality, etc.

6. Unemployment Claims, (ya Jobless Claims).. “Pichle week me kitne logon ne U.S. government se unemployment benefit (birozgaari allowance) ke liye apply kiya.” Simple words me: Kitne logon ne job lose ki aur government se paisa maanga jab unke paas kaam nahi tha.

7. Retail Sales, ye directly batati hai ki consumer spending (kharch) kitna strong hai — aur U.S. economy ka 70% hissa consumer spending par depend karta hai 💵.. “U.S. me pichle month me logon ne retail stores, restaurants, gas stations, online shops, etc. me kitna paisa kharch kiya.  Simple words me: Ye data batata hai ki log shopping kar rahe hain ya nahi 🛍️

8. Industrial Production, U.S. ke industrial sector (factories, mines, electricity utilities) me total goods production pichle month ke mukable me kitna badha ya ghata. Simple words me: Ye data batata hai ki industry kitna produce kar rahi hai — agar zyada production ho raha hai → economy strong 💪 agar production gir raha hai → slowdown ka signal
