## Trading Rules 

1. Strong, (Support/Resistance) ha Market Trap karega 

2. Week,   (Support/Resistance) ha Market Trap nahe karega

3. Market, (Up-Trand/Dow-Trand) ho SL kha kr Move deta h

4. Entry,  (Support, Registantion) par hi lee

5. Exit,   (Support/Registran) par hi lee

6. SL,     (5-7) Point... SL sa Target 3x hona chahiya

7. Target, (3x,4x) hona chahiya

8. Time,   (4H,1H,30M,15,5) First long time frame my market UP-Trande y Down-Trande h pata kare (4H,1H)...

   Medium Time Frame pe Market my Entry Exite Plane kare (15M)....
   Short Time Frame pe market my entry our exit lee (5M)....

9. session (U.K), (U.S.A), (Asia), Market different session my different behave karta hai.. Trade Session Time ka accoding Plan kare ..

10. Holding Trade (liquidity) market jabe v liquidity letee h tabe market hold krna chahiya

11. Market Short Time Frame (5m, 15m) my liquidity jama kr k fer use Hant kr k fer Ek Boom Run deta h like GOLD 300, pips 400, pips move deta h
