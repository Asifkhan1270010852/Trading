## Trading Rules 

1. Strong, (Support/Resistance) Market Trap karega..

2. Week,   (Support/Resistance) Market Trap nahe karega..

3. Market, (Up-Trand/Dow-Trand) ho SL kha kr Move deta h, Market pahle liquidity jama krta h use ko grap kr k fer apna Trande Follow krta hai..

4. Entry,  (Support, Registantion) par hi lee, Market pahle Chart-Patten bana tha h fer liquidity hunt krta h fer market my entry na lee..

5. Exit,   Market ko multi time frame my useka strengths check karo ager Strong strengths nahe h to market sa exit le loo..

6. SL,     SL sa Target 3x 4x ya 5x  hona chahiya..

7. Target, (3x,4x) hona chahiya..

8. Time,   (4H,1H,30M,15,5) First long time frame my market UP-Trande y Down-Trande h pata kare (4H,1H)...

   Medium Time Frame pe Market my Entry Exite Plane kare (15M)....
   Short Time Frame pe market my entry our exit lee (5M)....

9. session (U.K), (U.S.A), (Asia), Market different session my different behave karta hai.. Trade Session Time ka accoding Plan kare ..

10. Holding Trade (strengths) market jabe v Strong strengths dekhata h tabe market hold krna chahiya..

11. Market Short Time Frame (5m, 15m) my liquidity jama kr k fer use Hant kr k fer Ek Boom Run deta h like GOLD 300, pips 400, pips move deta h...
